How Weather Forecast Works

Weather forecasting uses scientific data collected from satellites, weather stations, and radars. These systems analyze temperature, humidity, wind, and pressure to predict future weather conditions.

Modern weather apps use advanced models to provide accurate hourly and daily forecasts.

Why Temperature Changes Daily

Temperature changes due to sunlight, air pressure, and seasonal variations. During the day, the sun heats the Earth's surface, increasing temperature. At night, heat escapes into the atmosphere, causing cooling.

Understanding Rain Prediction

Rain prediction is based on cloud formation, humidity levels, and atmospheric pressure. When moisture in the air condenses into clouds, it can lead to rainfall.

The percentage shown in weather apps indicates the probability of rain.

Importance of Checking Weather Daily

Checking weather helps you plan your day better. It is useful for travel, outdoor work, farming, and daily activities.
